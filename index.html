About Jessica Morrison

“Let's make friends with the cool kids just this once, she says, and doesn't blink at all.” — Hallie and Henry, Say Hi

Jessica Morrison is one part side-eye, and three parts just excited to be here. She used to write her bios in third person, but then one day I was like this is stupid.

I’m a product manager at Chemical & Engineering News, the weekly news magazine of the American Chemical Society. I used write about U.S. policy and how it affects the chemical enterprise. Now I help writers tell their stories by keeping things moving behind the scenes.

I'm excited about creating memorable user experiences, building community on-line and off, and changing the culture of work. Say hi. ❤︎
